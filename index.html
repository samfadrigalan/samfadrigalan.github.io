About

Hello, my name is Samantha Fadrigalan! I am a rising junior in Computer Science, with a minor in Math, at Louisiana State University. Geaux Tigers!

My journey in Computer Science started with my high school teacher in a small public boarding school in Louisiana. I took her Pre-Calculus class during the fall of junior year, and she later tried to convince me to take her C++ class the next semester. I refused. However, my opinion on that subject did not matter as she also happened to be my assigned advisor, and was in charge of my course schedule. I had planned on just taking the class for a week and somehow bypass her for the drop process. But, needless to say, I never did.

I enjoy enjoy volunteering and organizing outreach events. I'm a member of Society of Peer Mentors (SPM) at LSU, and I get to mentor local high school students during FIRST robotics season. I also co-founded a hackathon at LSU, GeauxHack, with tons of help from SPM. I'm also an officer of the very new Women in Computer Science (WICS) organization at LSU. A bunch of us from SPM and WICS are also planning on having a program to teach CS using Arduinos to secondary school girls. And I also recently became president of Association of Computing Machinery (ACM) at LSU. If you would like to come join us for an ACM meeting, inquire about GeauxHack, or anything regarding the above, feel free to contact me!

This summer, I will have my very first internship as a Consultant intern at Pariveda Solutions in Houston. I'm very excited to learn new things.

Aside from all things CS, I love music. I play guitar, piano, and sing unafraid of how I sound. I sometimes treat my musically talented friends as my personal band. I love tasting new foods. I like taking walks outside if it's just about 60°F and the sky is a shade of orange. I love learning about other cultures. I'm recentely entering a Marvel phase (MCU). I might delve into the comic books soon.

If you'd like to come visit an ACM meeting, inquire about GeauxHack, or anything above, feel free to contact me. If you would just like to say hi, you can contact me too! I love meeting people. If you want to look at my code for this website, checkout my Github. Thank you for reading this far. :)
